Trina Harding: Instructional Designer

Get to know Trina Harding

Trina is a second year masters student in Instructional Psychology and Technology at Brigham Young University. Her research interests include experiential learning, narrative learning theory, and k-12 innovation. Among her accomplishments are keeping small people alive for 21 years (and running), staying mostly fit without having to jog, and making a Halloween costume out of bubble wrap. Her skills include knowing the songs of any musical from the 1950's-1960's.
